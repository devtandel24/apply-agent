## About Placeholder Systems
Placeholder Systems provides an enterprise ML platform used by financial services
firms to manage model risk, automate retraining cycles, and ensure regulatory
compliance of ML-driven decisions.

## Role Overview
The MLOps Engineer will own the infrastructure and tooling that enables our data
science team to iterate quickly and ship models safely. You'll work at the
intersection of software engineering, DevOps, and machine learning.

## Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines for model training, evaluation, and deployment
- Manage our ML platform stack: MLflow, Kubeflow Pipelines, Docker, Kubernetes
- Implement automated model monitoring, drift detection, and alerting
- Develop infrastructure-as-code (Terraform) for ML compute environments on AWS
- Work with data scientists to productionize notebooks into robust pipelines

## Requirements
- 2+ years of MLOps, DevOps, or platform engineering experience
- Strong Python and shell scripting skills
- Experience with Docker and Kubernetes (EKS or GKE)
- Proficiency with a CI/CD tool (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, or CircleCI)
- Familiarity with MLflow or Kubeflow
- Must be authorized to work in the US without sponsorship now or in the future

## Nice to Have

- Knowledge of model governance and auditability requirements in regulated industries
