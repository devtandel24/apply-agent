# Data Engineer — Sample Data Co

**Location:** Austin, TX (On-site / Hybrid)
**Salary:** $95,000 – $130,000
**Posted:** 2026-06-11

## About Sample Data Co
Sample Data Co is a B2B SaaS company providing a customer data platform to
mid-market retailers. Our platform ingests, cleans, and activates customer data
across marketing, commerce, and support channels.

## Role Overview
We are hiring a Data Engineer to help scale our ingestion and transformation
pipelines. You will work on the core data infrastructure that powers our analytics
product and ML feature store.

## Responsibilities
- Design and build batch and streaming data pipelines (Kafka, Airflow, dbt)
- Maintain and optimize our data warehouse (Snowflake) and feature store
- Implement data quality checks, schema validation, and SLA monitoring
- Collaborate with ML engineers to ensure high-quality, low-latency feature delivery
- Optimize query performance and manage compute costs across cloud data services

## Requirements
- 2+ years of data engineering experience
- Proficiency in Python and SQL
- Hands-on experience with Airflow or a similar workflow orchestration tool
- Experience with a cloud data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift)
- Familiarity with streaming technologies (Kafka, Kinesis, or Flink)
- Strong understanding of data modeling, normalization, and partitioning strategies

## Nice to Have
- Experience with dbt (data build tool)
- AWS certifications or equivalent cloud experience
- Exposure to ML feature stores or real-time serving infrastructure

## What We Offer
- Competitive salary with annual performance bonus
- Hybrid work — 2 days/week in Austin office
- 401(k) with 4% employer match
- $1,500 technology stipend
