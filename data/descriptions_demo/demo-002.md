# Senior Machine Learning Engineer — Fictional AI Labs

**Location:** New York, NY (Remote OK)
**Salary:** $120,000 – $160,000
**Posted:** 2026-06-11

## About Fictional AI Labs
Fictional AI Labs is an applied AI research company building production-grade NLP
and computer vision systems for healthcare and finance verticals. We publish
regularly and open-source our infrastructure tooling.

## Role Overview
As a Senior ML Engineer you will own model development and serving infrastructure
end-to-end. You'll lead small sub-teams, set technical direction for ML systems,
and mentor junior engineers. The role is 70% engineering, 30% research.

## Responsibilities
- Lead design and implementation of large-scale ML training and inference systems
- Fine-tune and evaluate large language models for domain-specific classification
  and extraction tasks
- Build robust evaluation harnesses and benchmark suites for LLM quality assurance
- Partner with research scientists to move prototype models into production
- Drive MLOps best practices: experiment tracking (MLflow), model registry,
  CI/CD for models, automated evaluation pipelines

## Requirements
- 3+ years of ML engineering experience, including at least 1 year on LLM systems
- Expert-level Python; production experience with PyTorch
- Demonstrated experience fine-tuning or prompting transformer models (BERT, GPT,
  Llama, or similar)
- Strong background in experiment tracking and model lifecycle management
- Experience shipping models to production (containerization, serving APIs)
- MS or PhD in a quantitative field preferred

## Nice to Have
- Published work or open-source contributions in NLP/ML
- Experience with RAG architectures and vector databases (pgvector, FAISS, Pinecone)
- Familiarity with evaluation frameworks (HELM, MMLU, custom harnesses)

## What We Offer
- Above-market compensation + meaningful equity
- Remote-first culture; NY office available
